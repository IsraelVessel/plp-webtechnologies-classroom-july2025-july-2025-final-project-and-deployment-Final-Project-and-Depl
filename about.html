About Me

I am an interdisciplinary graduate applicant and researcher based in Nigeria, combining expertise in applied linguistics and computational tools to explore language variation and technology integration. Holding a Bachelor of Arts in English with a strong academic record, my current work involves detailed phonetic analysis and innovative use of computational methods and AI tools to deepen understanding of English varieties, notably Igbo English. Alongside my research, I develop scalable web applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python programming. I am adept at full-stack development workflows, experimental design, data analysis, and integrating large language models and AI frameworks for natural language processing. Driven by curiosity and a commitment to impactful scholarship, I produce clean, reproducible code and maintain meticulous documentation. I also enjoy creative writing, song drafting, and graphic design, enabling a rich, multidisciplinary approach to my projects.
